First Architectural Course

WAS HELD ON 8th of JULY 2023

Mattoni offers a unique opportunity for architects and undergraduate students to participate in an immersive learning experience focused on acquiring new techniques, tools, and practical skills in the field of design.

This prestigious event consists of multiple sections dedicated to various architectural fields, with step-by-step guidance offered in each class:

1. How to design interiors using 3D max, sketch-up and corona render with our professional staff.
2. How to render realistic exterior visualizations using (D5 + lumion)
3. How to use AI (artificial intelligence) in architecture to influence your work and use it in your projects
4. Site visit with our engineering staff to our projects.
5. Theoretical lectures about how to run your business and how to start designing for a client.
6. Introducing international and local companies in different fields of ceramic and materials and furniture and lighting.

Duration of the course: two months, three days a week, two hours a day.
